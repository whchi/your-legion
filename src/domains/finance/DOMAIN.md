# Finance Domain

Use this domain when the task involves pricing, runway, revenue, margin, costs, financial tradeoffs, scenario analysis, financial reporting shape, or business impact analysis.

Do not use this domain for accounting recognition rules unless the task asks for financial impact as well. Do not use it for implementation-only work unless financial calculation rules must be defined.
